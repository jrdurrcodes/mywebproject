about me

I, Dr. Janjala Durr native of Mississippi wear many hats. I am Barber Stylist, Motivational Enthusiast, Urban Farmer and Junior Developer. With twenty-four years under my belt as a Entreprenuer Barber Stylist, two and a half years as a farmer, the decision was made to pivot my career and try something completely out of my comfort zone, leading me to webpage development. Maintaining healthy high level goals allows me to strive continously to be better each day. It is my desire is not to keep this knowledge that acquired to myself, yet to utilize what I have learned to empower others to be the. It is also my hopes to inspire the people she encounters to never be satisfied with being mediocre. Through time, discipline and consistency I believe that all things are possible when one believes in themself. My plans are to form a non-profit as well as create future employment opportunites in various fields that I have been serving in for over 24 years.

MS Coding Academy has created a great opportunity to become a Junior Developer, with the award of a 6 month scholarship. Since joining the academy I have already learned and applied HTML, CSS, Coolors, Figma, Bootstraps, Google Fonts, Github and few other IT programs. I came in with zero knowledge of any of these programs and have since developed my own webpage among other projects shown here on my webpage. It is my hope to someday take this skill set to develop webpages for small businesses and farmers.
