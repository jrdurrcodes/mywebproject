about me

Dr. Janjala Durr native of Mississippi, is a Hair Stylist, Motivational Enthusiast, Urban Farmer and Junior Developer. With twenty-four years under her belt as a Barber Stylist, two and a half years as a farmer, she decided to pivot her career to webpage development. Her goals are to strive to better each day. Dr. Durr's desire is not just to keep this knowledge that she has acquired to herself, yet to utilize what she has learned to empower others to seek more knowledge. It is also her hopes to inspire the people she encounters to never be satisfied with being mediocre. Through time, discipline and consistency she believes that all things are possible when one believes in themself. Dr. Durr plans to form her own non-profit as well as create future employment opportunites in various fields that she has been serving in for over 24 years.
